Beaf lasagna recipe for the carnivarous human!

Lasagna will be enjoyed with healthy serving of beaf eagwhites floor souce. Meatball suprise "mines" will be added for succulent delight.

    Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Eggwhites
  • Meatballs
  • Minced meat
  • Milk
  • Cheese
    Steps
  1. Boil some pasta to hear cooked level
  2. Prepare the souce and paste as follows
      Souce
    1. Fry onion with vegetable oil
    2. Add tomato paste just after browning
    3. Add minced meat with hot water
    4. Simmer to tender
      Paste
    1. Mix eggwhites upto whitened into foam
    2. Mix with lukewarm milk
    3. Add oliveoil and mix
    4. Warm under low heat
  3. Pile the pasta souce and paste in layers
  4. Add meat balls at random for maximum suprise
  5. Cover with alluminum foil and bake to crisp
  6. Add cheese on top just before serving
